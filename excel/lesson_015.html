Lesson 14: Working with SUM Functions and Formulas in Excel

Objective: Students will be able knowledge of basic mathmatical operations to create formulas in Excel and determine when it is appropriate to utilize Functions for analysis.

What is the difference between these two statements?

  • =SUM(A3:A4)
  • =A3 + A4

If you answered that the first one is a FUNCTION and the second line is a FORMULA you are correct! Both complete the addition operation. The only difference is the FUNCTION can handle a range of numbers from different worksheets easier then a formula.
