Lesson 18: Excel VLookup and HLookup

Objective: Students will use the functions HLOOKUP and VLOOKUP in Excel to crossreference two tables and return similar data.

HLOOKUP and VLOOKUP.

Sometimes we want to take information from one table and place it into another table. Excel can find matching cells from two tables. This is what VLOOKUP and HLOOKUP both perform. The only difference is VLOOKUP is vertical table. So Excel looks vertically through the data. HLOOKUP, Excel will look horizontally.
