Lesson 5: Navigating through Workbooks and Worksheets

Objective: Students will be able to copy data from one workbook to another and navigate through the workbook using named ranges and the find/replace tool.

All Excel worksheets contain cells. Finding data in a large worksheet with hundreds of cells can be difficult. In this lesson, we are going learn how to organize our data and worksheets for easier navigation.
