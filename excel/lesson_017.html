Lesson 17: Working with Functions for Business

Objective: Students will use functions in Excel to determine NET Income and Profit Margin.

There are two business terms, or vocabulary words, you need to know for the MOS 2016 exam.

NET INCOME: is Net Sales minus Net costs. The word NET means total minus expenses.

PROFIT MARGIN: is the income made by a company after expenses, displayed as a percentage.

Most importantly, do not stress over these terms. Just be familar with them and break down which mathmatical operation the question is asking you to perform.
