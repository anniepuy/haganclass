Lesson 11: Introduction to Chart in ExcelSparklines_Subtotals

Objective: Students will to insert and format a column chart using existing data in Excel

Excel can contain hundreds of rows and columns of data. For people, having data organized is one thing, but it's useless if we cannot analyze the data. This is where charts in Excel are so amazing. In this lesson you will practice creating charts based on data stored in an Excel workbook. This is one of the most helpful tools to bring back to math and science class!
