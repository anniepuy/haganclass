MineCraft Farm Final Project

Objective: Students will build a virtual farm in MineCraft while utilizing Microsoft Excel to keep track of their business sales.

MineCraft Farm Instructions

In teams of four, you will be creating a virtual farm in MineCraft. Your farm will be focused on agritourism, in other words, open to the public to sell food, plants, and pet animals. Before creating your farm, you need to watch the following videos.

Post Video Discussion and Preassessment

Now that you have been introduced to Agritourism, take the preassessment quiz on Office 365.

Each group will consist of 4 people. Each person will have one of the following jobs:

  • Animal Activities - Desiging the part of the farm regarding the animal activities.
  • Flowering and gardening activities - Desiging the the flowers and gardens area that will be used by the food activities.
  • Structural Items - Designing the farm, paths, signs, and buildings on the farm.
  • Food Activities - Designing the food items, crafting the baked / cooked good, etc.

Now discuss and record with group the health, security, buildings, and farming concerns. Be sure to take notes for your PowerPoint.

Planning the Farm

Now you are ready to plan together the layout of the farm. One person will create an Excel file online. They will share the link with their partners. Each person in the group will help design the layout. Your farm in MineCraft should resemble your plan. Watch my video on how to create your layout design.

Post Building Requirements

After you build your MineCraft farm, you will need use Excel and PowerPointto complete these items:

  • Excel table tracking the amount of food sales, flowers, plants, and animals you have.
  • Excel table with projected revenue from the Farm. Your group will need to set the prices of your items.
  • PowerPoint to create an information presentation about your farm. Your audience will be prospective clients.
  • PowerPoint to create an reflective analysis of your farm and the operations. You must include the orignial health, security, building, and farm ground concerns. Discuss any team managment concerns or successes. Your audience for this presentation is your investor - Mrs. Hagan.
