Lesson 7: Using Absolute and Relative References with Finanical Spreadsheets

Objective: Students will learn how to use Min, Max, and Sum Functions with both relative and abosolute reference.

In Excel, we can reference cells that contain similar or related information.

There are two types of cell references: relative and absolute.

Relative references change when a formula is copied to another cell. Absolute references, on the other hand, remain constant no matter where they are copied.
