Lesson 1: What is Microsoft Excel?

Objective: Students will be able to identify business uses for electronic spreadsheets.

Excel is an electronic spreadsheet program. A spreadsheet is just an electronic list. Most importantly, electronic spreadsheets can perform numeric calculations quickly and accurately. Some basic terms you need to know:

  1. Workbook: The entire excel file.
  2. Worksheet: The individual sheet inisde the Excel file. Each workbook may have several worksheets.
