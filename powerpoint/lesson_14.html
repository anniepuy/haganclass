2016 Microsoft Office PowerPoint Certification

Below is informiton on how students can certify at home for PowerPoint 2016

Requirements to be eligbile to ceritfy in PowerPoint

  1. Student has completed all of the SAM Cengage PowerPoint trainings and Exams with a 85% or better.
  2. Student has completed the SAM Cengage 2016 MOS PowerPoint Practice Exam with an 85% or better
  3. Student is currently certified in Microsoft Word and Excel, or wants to retake the Excel or Word certification.
  4. Student's technology meets Certiport criteria for remote testing. See Certiport's website.

Certiport Criteria

Students and family member sshould carefully read and watch Certiport's instructions. More information can be found on Certiport homepage.

Testing Appointments

While tests are taken at home, they are administered with a proctor online. This requires a set appointment time. Each appointment time is reserved for the student, cannot be changed or adjusted. Students receive one appointment time each.

Students that do not show for their testing time, will not get another chance to test.
