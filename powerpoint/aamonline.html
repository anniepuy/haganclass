"All About Me" Online Presentation Requirements

Objective: To demonstrate competent PowerPoint skills.

How will you achieve this objective?

Create a presentation about yourself. Include such items as: Hobby, Sport, Family, Work, School, Future Plans (college, career, etc.), anything appropriate you would like to share.

These instructions for for the online version only. Be sure to turn OFF the “Simplified” ribbon bar. See below picture..

Minimum Requirements

  1. Save presentation in your DIT working folder-> 365 OneDrive account as: “PP_Me_your last name” example: PP_Me_Hagan.pptx)
  2. Title slide with title and your name
  3. Include a minimum of 2 pictures - this can be of you, your family, pets, or a stock image of something you enjoy such as a hobby or sport.
  4. Minimum of 8 slides
  5. Include one slide with listing the topics you will cover in the presentation
  6. Slide #3 should include your name, class period, and date. Hide this slide.
  7. Use a different transition on all sides.
  8. Animation on at least 4 different objects in the PowerPoint such as a picture, smart art, words, etc.
  9. Family slide which uses a SmartArt of your family tree, pets, or your favorite items. I need to see you use a SmartArt in some method.
  10. Ending slide
  11. Use a themed background - not distracting with your images..
  12. Include alt text on each one of your pictures, SmartArts, or tables (if you included one). Be sure to add a title and description for each alt text.
  13. On each slide, use at least 3 bullets of information. (5-7 words each - NO PARAGRAPHS)
  14. Utilize a different slide layout on at least three of the eight slides. Do not use the same layout for the entire presentation.
  15. Have all material related to you, the topic.
  16. Add your name and class period in the notes area for the first slide.
  17. Spell check the entire document.
  18. Create a PDF by "printing" the handouts. Be sure to save it as YOURLASTNAME_FIRSTNAME_PPAM.pdf Please share the document with me.

The criterion listed above is required. You can certainly share more— this is your opportunity to be creative and show me what you have learned.
